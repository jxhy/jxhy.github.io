Personal Profile

Li Qi, Ph.D., associate researcher, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, member of the Visual Big Data Committee of the Chinese Society of Image and Graphics,
and member of the Pattern Recognition Committee of the Chinese Society of Artificial Intelligence. From 2011 to 2016, he received his Ph.D degree from the State Key Laboratory
of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. The main research directions include computer vision, face preprocessing and recognition, etc., presided over 1 project of the National
Natural Science Foundation, as the actual person in charge, undertook the sub-projects of the National Key Research and Development Program, the National Natural Science Foundation of China,
the Beijing Natural Science Foundation of China, etc. Scientific research projects, participating face recognition systems have been successfully applied to banks and railway systems.
Published many articles in mainstream international journals and conferences, applied for many patents, and is currently a reviewer for TPAMI, PR, NeurIPS, ICML, etc.

Research Areas

Computer vision, biometric recognition, face preprocessing and recognition
